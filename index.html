The Rise Of A.I

2020-2040

Artificial Intelligence is already transforming our lives. We are surrounded by semi-autonomous cars, robotic machines, smart biometrics which are just a few examples of AI used in technologies around us.


Artificial Intelligence is getting better and will be constantly evolving and impacting almost every area such as education, healthcare, entertainment, governance, public safety etc. With more adoption and innovation it has the potential to improve the quality of human life significantly.


Taylor Swift(Popular Singer at our time) used facial recognition to track predators at music concerts(People used to gather at a place to watch her sing). She’s not the only one. Facial recognition is rolling out to airports everywhere all over the world and it’s coming to a street corner near you.
